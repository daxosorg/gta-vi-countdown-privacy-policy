microphone, or files. We also do not use any analytics or other third-party SDKs that collect your usage
data. The only data ever used by the App is non-sensitive technical information for advertising (see
Advertising and AdMob below). Crucially, we do not store any user information on any servers we
control.

Third-Party Services and Advertising
2
Our App is ad-supported and uses Google AdMob to display banner ads, interstitial ads, and native
ads. Google AdMob collects certain device and usage information (such as your device’s advertising ID,
IP address, and basic app usage data) to serve and target ads. We do not use any other advertising
networks or third-party ad providers. We have no affiliate or ad revenue partnerships beyond Google
AdMob. Aside from advertising, no other third-party services or SDKs are integrated into the App.

Google AdMob: We use AdMob for all ads. AdMob may collect non-personal device identifiers
(e.g. Advertising ID) and coarse location (from IP address) to optimize ad delivery.
Ad Formats: The App shows banner ads (small ads at top/bottom), interstitial ads (full-screen
between screens), and native ads (customized ad placements) via AdMob.
No Other Third Parties: We do not include any social media plugins, analytics tools, or
additional SDKs.

Personalized Ads and User Consent
3
Google’s policies and EU law require that we obtain user consent before using personal data for
personalized advertising in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK. Our App implements
Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request consent from EEA/UK users. When you first run
the App in the EEA/UK, you will see a consent dialog explaining how Google and we use your data for
ads. You can choose to allow or deny personalized ads. If you consent, AdMob will serve ads based on
your device’s Advertising ID and other non-sensitive data. If you do not consent (or if you are outside
EEA/UK), you will only see non-personalized ads (generic ads not based on your profile).
Google requires a certified Consent Management Platform for personalized ads in the EU/UK comply by using UMP to manage and record your choices. In summary:

Data Usage
We (Daxos Software) do not use your data for any purposes beyond serving ads. Specifically, we do not
sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. The only purpose of the data collected
via AdMob is ad targeting as described above. We do not perform any profiling beyond what AdMob
does automatically (and you can opt out of that by revoking consent). We do not email you marketing
messages, nor do we link any device data to your identity.
